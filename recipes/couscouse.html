Enduja-Pasta

Description

Easy to make, healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Couscouse
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Katama olives
  • Feta

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Pour over couscous
  3. Cut cucumber
  4. Cut tomato
  5. Cut feta
  6. Cut katama olives in two
  7. Mix everything together
