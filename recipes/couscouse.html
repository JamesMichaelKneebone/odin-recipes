Enduja-Pasta

Description

Easy, healthy and delicious.

Ingredients

  • Couscouse
  • Cucumber
  • Tomato
  • Katama olives
  • Feta

Steps

  1. Boil water pour a little bit over couscouse in a bowl/li>
  2. Cut cucumber
  3. Cut tomato
  4. Cut feta
  5. Cut katama olives in two
  6. Mix everything together
