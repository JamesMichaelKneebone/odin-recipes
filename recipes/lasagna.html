Lasagna

Description

Pasta but in layers

Ingredients

  • Lasagna plates
  • Tomato sauce
  • White onion
  • Cream
  • Oregano

Steps

  1. Turn on oven
  2. Make tomato sauce
  3. Make construction out of pasta layers
  4. Add sauce and cream
  5. Add oregano
